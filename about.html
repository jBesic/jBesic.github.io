About Me — Jasmin Besic

My name is Jasmin Besic and I’m a Software Engineer living in the Bosnia and Herzegovina. My interest in web development and software engineering started back in 2015 - I land a job as a junior engineer and my first project was Drupal theming and that taught me a lot about HTML & CSS & JS & PHP.

About This Blog

This blog is my platform for making notes and sharing gained knowledge with the community. With each post, I try to delve deeper into the ever-expanding universe of web development. I understand that growing my skills as a engineer not only takes time and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge, it also takes the support of others. I do my best to promote high-quality posts from which everyone in the community can learn.

I strongly believe that inspiration is for amateurs. So every day, I wake up, go to my desk, and actively "practice" web development for couple hours.

I try to publish several blog entries every week on some aspect of web application development including (but not limited to) the topics of JavaScript, Angular, React, HTML, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), PHP and SQL.
